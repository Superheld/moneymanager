The signature year curve — solid ink Ist, dashed teal Plan, "heute" marker, Engpass dip. Use on Übersicht and Liquiditätsplaner.

```jsx
<LiquidityChart plan={[4200,3800,5200,4600,4100,2600,1500,2400,3300,2900,3700,3100]}
                istThrough={5} dip={6} />
```

Always: Ist solid `--ink`, Plan dashed `--accent`. Pass 12 monthly balances. Wrap in a `<Card>` with a legend.
